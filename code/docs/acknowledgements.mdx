## Accessibility
This tool was developed by people without disabilities necessitating the use of screen readers, braille readers, or other assistive technologies. While we have done our best to understand the lived experiences of people with disabilities and existing inaccessible practices on the web, we may have made errors that further contribute to barriers to accessible perception and use. If you encounter any such barrier, please feel free to [share feedback, input, and concerns](/feedback) so we can continue to improve the tool and ensure access for as many people as possible!

## Locale
This tool was developed in Bloomington, Indiana. We wish to acknowledge and honour the Indigenous communities native to this region and recognise that Indiana University Bloomington is built on Indigenous homelands and resources. We recognise the [*myaamiaki*](https://miamination.com/) (Miami), [*Lënape*](https://delawaretribe.org/) (Delaware), [*Bodwéwadmik*](https://www.potawatomi.org/) (Potawatomi), and [*saawanwa*](https://shawnee-nsn.gov/) (Shawnee) people as past, present, and future caretakers of this land.
