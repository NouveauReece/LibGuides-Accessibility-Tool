## What is the LAT?
The LibGuides Accessibility Tool (LAT) is a bookmarklet that runs right within your web browser, helping uncover accessibility violations on your LibGuides and suggesting ways to resolve violations using the LibGuides Rich Text Editor.

## Installing LAT
To install, visit the [Installation Page](/install) and follow the directions for your browser.

## What's a Bookmarklet?
A *[bookmarklet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bookmarklet)* (also sometimes called a *favelet*) is similar to a browser extension: they both let you add features to your browser or to websites you visit. Unlike an extension, bookmarklets live in your browser's bookmarks bar and run only when you click on the bookmarklet's icon in your bookmarks bar. And because LibGuides Accessibility Tool is a bookmarklet that lives directly in your web browser's bookmarks bar, there's no extra desktop application you need to install.
